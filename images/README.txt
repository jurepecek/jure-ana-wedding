HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
=======================

Simply add your photos to this folder with the following names:

- photo1.jpg
- photo2.jpg
- photo3.jpg
- photo4.jpg
- photo5.jpg
- photo6.jpg

Tips:
- Use JPG or JPEG format for best compatibility
- Recommended size: 800-1200px width for good quality and fast loading
- The photos will automatically crop to fit the gallery layout
- You can add more photos by editing index.html and adding more gallery-item divs

If you want to use different filenames, just update the src="" paths in index.html
